Computer Recycling

Items We Collect...

Expert Computer Disposals Ltd collect and recycle various equipment such as listed below.
Computers

We collect Desktop Computers of any age and in any condition.

LCD Monitors

We collect flatscreen LCD Monitors of any size or shape.

Laptops

Laptops, Netbooks, MacBooks or others, we collect them all.

Servers

We collect Desktop / Rackmount Servers, including rails.

Mice

100's of Mice in the cupboard? It's not a problem for us, we are happy to collect them.

Tablets

We collect tablets or any make, model or size, including non-working or broken screens.

Switches

Yep, we collect and recycle any Network related IT equipment you may have.

Projectors

Expert Computer Disposals Ltd collect and recycle both floor standing and portable projectors.

Keyboards

Don't worry if you have a lot of keyboards, we are happy to remove them.

Cables

Knotted Cables? We will also remove all of your used cables, even if they are all tied together.

What happens to the Equipment? ...

One of the most important factors of recycling is reuse.

Your recycled equipment will have the data securely erased, the equipment will then be inspected, cleaned up and refurbished and if possible resent back out into the world.

We also supply equipment to schools / academies at a reduced expense.


Paperwork? ...

Along with a great service you will receive a Waste Transfer Note for your electrical waste which demonstrates you have correctly disposed of your equipment.

If you have any items that require data erasure, you will also receive a Data Destruction Certificate.
