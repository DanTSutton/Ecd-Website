About Us

Established

Expert Computer Disposals Ltd was established in May 2012, since then, we have gone on to help and assist many Businesses, Schools, Academies, Universities, Health Authorities, Solicitors, Accountants and many more in their recycling duties to make our world a cleaner and greener place to live.

We have a legal duty to recycle electrical equipment in the correct way and consider the welfare of our future generations.

Landfill Policy

We pride ourselves in going the extra mile with helping and guiding businesses in any aspect of any electrical recycling. From the beginning we have maintained a strong commitment of a 0% landfill policy.
